- **Something for You** - Main menu with three options:

## ?? How to Use

1. **Open the site**: Simply open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Navigate**: Click through the pages using buttons and cards
3. **Music**: Click the ?? button in the bottom-right to toggle background music
4. **Gallery**: View memories in the polaroid-style gallery
5. **Video**: Watch the special video on the "Our Story" page

## ?? Pages

1. **Page 1 - Proposal**: "Will you be mine?" with animated video
2. **Page 2 - Happy Propose Day**: Celebration page with animated video
3. **Page 3 - Something for You**: Main menu with three feature cards
4. **Gallery**: Photo gallery with 6 polaroid memories
5. **Letter**: Personal letter "My Heart to Yours"
6. **Video**: "Just for You" video player
7. **Final**: "Forever to Go!" closing message

## ??? Technologies Used

## ?? Customization

To customize the site:
- Replace images in `assets/images/`
- Replace videos in `assets/videos/`
- Replace audio in `assets/music/`
- Modify text content in `index.html`
- Adjust colors and styling in `style.css`
